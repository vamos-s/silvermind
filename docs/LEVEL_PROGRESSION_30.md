## Progression Difficulty Tiers

### 🟢 Levels 1-5: Easy Introduction
- Beginner-friendly pace
- Clear visual feedback
- Generous time limits

### 🟡 Levels 6-10: Medium Challenge
- Noticeable difficulty increase
- Requires more concentration
- Moderate time pressure

### 🟠 Levels 11-20: Advanced
- Significant challenge
- Requires developed skills
- Limited time for decisions

### 🔴 Levels 21-30: Expert
- Extreme difficulty
- Requires mastery
- Minimal time allowed
