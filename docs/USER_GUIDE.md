## 🎮 Game Categories

### 1. Memory Games
**Goal**: Improve your memory retention and recall ability.

#### Number Recall
- **How to Play**: Watch a sequence of numbers, then type them in order
- **Tip**: Break long sequences into chunks
- **Progression**: 10 levels, increasing sequence length

#### Sequence Memory
- **How to Play**: Watch cells light up in a pattern, then repeat it
- **Tip**: Focus on the order, not just the positions
- **Progression**: 10 levels, larger grids and longer sequences

#### Word Recall
- **How to Play**: Memorize a list of words, then type them
- **Tip**: Group related words together
- **Progression**: 10 levels, more words and harder vocabulary

#### Card Flip
- **How to Play**: Match pairs of cards by flipping them
- **Tip**: Remember the positions of each card
- **Progression**: 5 levels, more cards and less time

---

### 2. Logic Games
**Goal**: Enhance your problem-solving and logical reasoning skills.

#### Logic Puzzle
- **How to Play**: Use clues to arrange boxes from smallest to largest
- **Tip**: Start with the most restrictive clues
- **Progression**: 10 levels, more boxes and fewer clues

#### Sudoku
- **How to Play**: Fill the grid with numbers 1-9 without repeats
- **Tip**: Start with rows/columns with most numbers filled
- **Progression**: 3 grid sizes (4×4, 6×6, 9×9)

---

### 3. Pattern Games
**Goal**: Improve pattern recognition and completion abilities.

#### Pattern Recognition
- **How to Play**: Identify the pattern and select what comes next
- **Tip**: Look for changes in color, shape, size, or position
- **Progression**: 10 levels, longer patterns and more options

#### Sequence Completion
- **How to Play**: Complete the sequence by filling in missing items
- **Tip**: Identify the rule governing the sequence
- **Progression**: Coming soon

#### Maze Navigation
- **How to Play**: Find your way through the maze to the exit
- **Tip**: Use landmarks to remember paths
- **Progression**: Coming soon

#### Jigsaw Puzzle
- **How to Play**: Arrange pieces to complete the picture
- **Tip**: Start with edge pieces and corners
- **Progression**: Coming soon

---

### 4. Reaction Games
**Goal**: Improve your reaction time and reflexes.

#### Color Match
- **How to Play**: Match either the text color or word meaning
- **Tip**: Read the instruction carefully each time
- **Progression**: 10 levels, more colors and less time

#### Timing Game
- **How to Play**: Click when the screen turns green
- **Tip**: Don't click too early - wait for the change
- **Progression**: 10 levels, shorter windows of opportunity

#### Target Detection
- **How to Play**: Find and click all target items quickly
- **Tip**: Remaining targets shuffle after each click
- **Progression**: More targets and obstacles

#### Quick Reaction
- **How to Play**: Click as fast as possible when the screen turns green
- **Tip**: Keep your cursor/finger ready
- **Progression**: 10 levels, faster targets and shorter delays

---

### 5. Spatial Games
**Goal**: Improve spatial awareness and visualization.

#### Rotated Shapes
- **How to Play**: Identify the rotated version of a shape
- **Tip**: Visualize the rotation in your mind
- **Progression**: More complex shapes and smaller angles

#### Distance Judgment
- **How to Play**: Estimate distances between points
- **Tip**: Use grid lines as reference
- **Progression**: More points and trickier configurations

## 📊 Understanding Your Progress

### Scoring System
Each game has its own scoring system, but most follow these principles:

- **Level Score**: Points earned for completing a level
- **Total Score**: Cumulative score across all levels
- **Time Bonus**: Extra points for fast completion
- **Accuracy Bonus**: Extra points for fewer mistakes

### Performance Metrics

#### Average Score
Your typical performance across all games

#### Best Score
Your highest score in each game

#### Streak
Consecutive days of training

#### Level Progress
How far you've progressed in each game's level system

### Viewing Statistics
1. Go to the Stats page
2. Select a category or game
3. View detailed graphs and metrics

## 🎯 Training Tips

### Daily Practice
- **Recommended**: 15-30 minutes per day
- **Best Time**: When you're alert and focused
- **Consistency**: Regular practice is better than long sessions

### Focused Training
- **Identify Weaknesses**: Check stats for low-scoring areas
- **Target Practice**: Spend extra time on weak areas
- **Balanced Training**: Don't neglect any category

### Maximizing Improvement
- **Challenge Yourself**: Play at higher difficulty levels
- **Review Mistakes**: Learn from wrong answers
- **Take Breaks**: Rest between intense sessions

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: How do I improve my scores?
**A**: Practice regularly, focus on weak areas, and try higher difficulty levels.

### Q: Can I track my progress over time?
**A**: Yes! Visit the Stats page to see detailed performance metrics.

### Q: Are these games scientifically validated?
**A**: Our games are based on established cognitive training principles, though individual results may vary.

### Q: How often should I play?
**A**: For best results, play 15-30 minutes daily.

### Q: Is my data private?
**A**: Yes! Your performance data is stored securely and never shared without consent.

### Q: Can I play offline?
**A**: Yes! Once loaded, all games work offline. (PWA feature coming soon)

## 📖 Glossary

- **Working Memory**: Short-term memory used for reasoning and decision-making
- **Pattern Recognition**: Ability to identify patterns in data or visual information
- **Reaction Time**: Time taken to respond to a stimulus
- **Spatial Awareness**: Understanding where objects are in space
- **Cognitive Reserve**: The mind's resistance to damage of the brain
